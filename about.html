Come dine at Cafe Cougar !


Cafe Cougar is the lastest addition to the University of Houston Sugarland Campus. We are a cafeteria catering to all students, faculty and visitors. Our menu consists of delicious nutritional meals offerred at an affordable price. Cafe Cougar is proudly run by Ronald Nguyen and his staff of esteemed chefs. To learn more about the head chef and owner, click here. We are located on the first floor of Brazos Hall, come visit us today!
